Easy Eton Mess

Description

Eton mess is an incredibly simple yet delicious British dessert made in minutes by combining chunks of

meringue cookies with whipped cream and fresh berries. Spoon into glasses for a sensational summer treat!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 4 store-bought meringue cookies
  • 12 ounces ripe raspberries

Steps

  1. Beat cream in a chilled glass or metal bowl with an electric mixer until soft peaks form.

  2. Break meringue cookies into bite-sized chunks and add to whipped cream; fold

    in berries lightly with a metal spoon until you see streaks of dark pink but still

    retain some chunks of meringue.

  3. Spoon Eton mess into four glass dishes and serve.
